Engineered Efficiency

A synchronized 3-month construction workflow designed for the modern Owner Builder.

01

Phase I: Site Feasibility & Variable Assessment

Every project begins with a deep-dive feasibility study. We evaluate access, total backyard area, and site conditions. While our modular units represent a fixed manufacturing cost, other variables are site-dependent.

Fixed Cost Element

  • Prefabricated Structure
  • In-factory Fit-out
  • Standard Architectural Specs

Variable Estimates

  • Soil-specific Engineering
  • Distance to Utility Mains
  • Council Overlays/Permit Fees
02

Phase II: Professional Facilitation & Permits

To ensure absolute compliance and avoid conflict of interest, Waverley Granny Flat utilizes a network of independent third-party consultants. Our team assists with the complex paperwork, while licensed Architects, Structural Engineers, and Building Surveyors provide the necessary certifications.

Total Peace of Mind: We coordinate the entire submission process—including Soil Tests, Engineering Designs, and Council Report & Consent (if required)—through verified independent professionals.
03

Phase III: 10-Week Synchronized Production

Once the Building Permit is issued, our unique dual-track timeline begins. This 10-week cycle maximizes efficiency by working on two continents simultaneously.

• Global Logistics (4-6 Weeks): While your unit is in transit via sea freight.

• Simultaneous Onsite Works: During the shipping window, we facilitate Foundations and Underground Services installation at your property. By the time the unit arrives, the site is 100% ready for mounting.

04

Phase IV: 2-Week Final Installation

With the unit on-site and foundations ready, the final structural assembly and service connections are typically completed within 2 weeks. This phase focuses purely on precision assembly and final utility tie-ins (Plumbing & Electrical).

Terms & Contingencies:
The 3-month timeline is a target based on smooth logistical and site execution. All timeframes are subject to prevailing Weather Conditions and Force Majeure events, including but not limited to: International Customs Inspections, Global Shipping Disruptions, or specialized Council Permit delays. Waverley Granny Flat remains committed to prompt communication and proactive problem-solving throughout the project duration.
