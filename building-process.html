The 3-Month Synchronized Roadmap

01

Phase I: Site Feasibility

Every project starts with a detailed assessment of area, access, and conditions. While modular components represent a fixed cost, we assist owners in identifying variables such as site-specific excavation and utility mains distance.

02

Phase II: Professional Separation & Permits

To maintain the highest integrity and strictly adhere to Victorian building regulations regarding conflicts of interest, Waverley Granny Flats operates with total professional separation. While our team provides technical guidance, we facilitate a direct bridge between owners and independent third-party consultants.

These licensed Architects and Structural Engineers handle specialized designs and soil testing, ensuring all documentation is prepared correctly for submission to the Relevant Building Surveyor (RBS) for statutory approval.

03

Phase III: 10-Week Production & Logistics

Upon RBS approval, the 10-week cycle begins. During the 4-6 week international shipping window, we facilitate simultaneous onsite groundworks, ensuring the foundations are ready before the unit arrives.

04

Phase IV: 2-Week Structural Assembly

Final installation and service connections (Plumbing/Electrical) typically take 2 weeks, focusing on precision assembly by licensed trades.
