THE 3-MONTH WORKFLOW

Phase 1: Feasibility & Independent Consultation

We begin with a site feasibility assessment. To ensure professional integrity, we facilitate coordination with independent third-party consultants (including licensed Surveyors and Engineers) who assist with the documentation and technical paperwork required for Building Permit applications.

Phase 2: 10-Week Synchronized Production

Upon permit issuance, the 10-week production cycle commences. This includes approximately 4-6 weeks of international transit. To maximize efficiency, onsite foundation and underground works are facilitated simultaneously during the shipping window, ensuring the site is ready for immediate installation.

Phase 3: 2-Week Structural Installation

Once the components arrive at your property, the pure installation phase typically takes 2 weeks. This includes the structural mounting and final utility connections (Plumbing/Electrical) by licensed trades.

Contingency Notice: The 3-month timeline is achievable under optimal conditions. All timeframes are subject to Weather Conditions and Force Majeure events (e.g., International Customs Inspections, Logistics disruptions, or Global conflicts). Waverley Granny Flats is committed to transparent updates should any external disruption occur.
