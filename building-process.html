THE 3-MONTH FRAMEWORK

01

Phase I: Feasibility & Assessment

We begin with a thorough site feasibility assessment. To ensure professional compliance, third-party consultants assist owners in evaluating site-specific variables—such as soil composition and utility mains distance—which determine the final engineering requirements.

02

Phase II: Professional Facilitation

Integrity is paramount. All Building Permit applications are assisted by a team of independent third-party professionals (Architects, Engineers, and Building Surveyors). They manage the technical certifications, providing you with a "Peace of Mind" framework while maintaining the objective oversight required by Victorian law.

03

Phase III: 10-Week Synchronized Cycle

Once the permit is issued, our 10-week synchronized production begins. This timeline is optimized for speed and precision:

• Factory & Transit (4-6 Weeks): High-precision manufacturing followed by international transit.

• Onsite Groundworks: Simultaneously, your property's foundations and underground services are installed. By the time the unit reaches your site, the ground is 100% prepared for mounting.

04

Phase IV: 2-Week Final Assembly

With the prefab components on site, the structural installation and utility tie-ins (Plumbing & Electrical) typically take just 2 weeks. This clean, efficient onsite phase minimizes disruption to your daily life.

CONTINGENCIES & TRANSPARENCY

The 3-month timeline reflects a standard smooth-execution project. All timeframes are subject to Weather Conditions and Force Majeure events—such as international customs inspections, logistics disruptions, or global conflicts. Waverley Granny Flats is committed to proactive communication and swift resolution of any external delays.
