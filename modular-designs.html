The 60m² Planning Advantage

Under Victoria’s Small Second Dwelling reforms, units under 60m² typically do not require a Planning Permit in residential zones. This creates a powerful opportunity for homeowners to expand rapidly.

At Waverley Granny Flats, we ensure this process is seamless. While you maintain ownership of the project, our professional third-party consultants assist with the paperwork to navigate technical compliance and Building Permit requirements.

Third-Party Expertise

To maintain the highest standards of integrity and avoid conflict of interest, all certifications—including Architectural, Structural, and Building Surveying—are facilitated via independent licensed professionals. They assist you in securing a full suite of permits for complete peace of mind.
