The 60m² Planning Advantage

Under Victoria’s Small Second Dwelling reforms, units under 60m² typically do not require a Planning Permit. At Waverley Granny Flat, we leverage this to accelerate your project.

Our expert consultants manage the full Building Permit process, ensuring your prefab addition is 100% compliant with local council and NCC 2022 standards.

Why Our "Component" Prefab?

"Prefab" at Waverley means structural precision. By manufacturing frames and panels in a controlled environment, we eliminate weather delays and ensure a 7-star energy seal that is impossible to achieve with traditional onsite framing.
