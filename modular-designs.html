Planning Permit Exemptions

Under Victoria’s latest Small Second Dwelling provisions, units under 60m² typically do not require a Planning Permit in residential zones. This creates a highly efficient path for homeowners to add secondary residences.

Waverley Granny Flats provides a framework that assists owners with the technical requirements. All Building Permits are facilitated through independent third-party consultants to ensure professional and objective compliance with all Victorian regulations.

Why Choose Modular?

By utilizing factory-controlled precision engineering, we eliminate common onsite variables. Our system ensures a 7-star energy rating and a superior structural finish compared to traditional onsite framing.
