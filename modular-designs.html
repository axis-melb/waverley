Why Factory-Built?

Traditional onsite builds depend on weather, multiple trades showing up on time, and site-by-site material decisions. Factory production eliminates all of that variability — every panel is cut, fitted, and inspected under controlled conditions before it leaves the factory.

The result is a more consistent finish, faster installation, and a structure that meets 7-star energy efficiency from day one.

What's included in every model

All models are delivered with structural framing, external cladding, internal lining, insulation to 7-star standard, windows and doors, kitchen and bathroom fit-out, and flooring. Site connections (plumbing, electrical, drainage) are completed by licensed trades during installation.
